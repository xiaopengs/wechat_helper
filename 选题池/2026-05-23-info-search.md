## Follow-Builders Digest 核心内容

### MAD Podcast: Yann Dubois (OpenAI Post-Training Frontiers)
**主题: "Why AI Progress Suddenly Feels Real"** (May 21)

核心观点:
- **可靠性阈值已跨过**: "You need to reach this level of reliability to really make any AI tools very useful, and I think we just crossed that probably December last year"
- **RL从竞赛到真实世界**: 过去RL只在math/coding等可验证奖励上训练，现在已转移到真实使用场景优化用户效用
- **效率革命**: GPT-5.5核心突破不是能力更强，而是「效率2X提升」——同样性能思考时间减半
- **Pre-training未碰壁**: "Larger models are actually giving you a lot of efficiency" — 更大模型思考需要更少tokens
- **世界模型谨慎**: "The problem is simulations are always going to be hard and not going to be truthful"
- **持续学习仍是未解决问题**: "continual learning remains one of the big unsolved problems in AI three years after ChatGPT"
